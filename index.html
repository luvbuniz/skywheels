City • Airport • Gas Station • Animals • Monster Cave

Drive fancy flying cars, walk on sidewalks, visit the airport & shops (enter only through doors!), fill up at the gas station, watch foxes & birds, and don’t wake the cave monster!

QUICK CONTROLS

  • WASD — drive or walk
  • F — get out / get in car · E enter door / use gas
  • SPACE fly up · SHIFT fly down (in car)
  • Watch fuel! Buy a gas can if you run out ⛽

📱 TOUCH CONTROLS

  • 🕹 Touch the left side — a circle appears under your finger. Push up to GO, sideways to turn
  • 👆 Drag anywhere else — look around
  • ⬆️ FLY · ⬇️ DOWN · 🛑 STOP — big buttons on the right
  • 🚶 get out / in the car · ⛽ doors, gas & shopping
  • ⌨️ A keyboard also works (WASD + Space)

💾 Progress saves on this tablet only · 📴 Works offline after one online load

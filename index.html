Fancy Flying Cars • City • Mountain • Tunnel

Cruise the neon city, zoom through a mountain tunnel, soar over the big bridge, and take to the sky whenever you want. Other fancy cars are driving and flying all around you!

HOW TO PLAY

  • WASD or arrows — drive / steer
  • SPACE — fly up   SHIFT — fly down
  • C — look behind you
  • V — switch camera (chase / driver’s seat)
  • R — reset if you get stuck
